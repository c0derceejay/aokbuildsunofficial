Welcome to AoK Builds!

Arena of Kings (AoK) is an online PvP game developed by Villain Games. Choose your class, customize your spells, and jump straight into 3v3 arena battles in a fight to the death! Win and be rewarded with epic loot and a global announcement of your glory to the Arena of Kings universe.

Endless Player Vs. Player battles!

Games are played as 3v3 team battles. If you win, you continue on to your next opponent -- you're part of a living tournament that is always running. If you lose, you get bounced back to the lobby screen. The game queue is structured like a pyramid.
